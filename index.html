About my portfolio

In this webpage, you could find all of my art works during term 1; durig term 1 and the start of term 2, we've learned the most basic skills of sketching- outlines, edges& shadings. We've also drew some basic shapes such as cubes, apple and a water bottle (which trains o measuring proportios without any tools like rulers); as our skills got a bit more advanced, we've drew real life objexts such as pots and fruits; by the end of term 1, with knowing most of the basic techniques in sketching, we've drew Pablo Picasso's Bull (which is my best piece of art work in for now I guess...). At the start of term 2, we've been trining on paintig, which is the hardest thing for me to do in art. Please click on. Due to my brain is insufficiently cultivated to comprehend the core skills of sketching and painting, so I have a limited artistic ability, please forgive me for some poorly painted/sketched drawings.
